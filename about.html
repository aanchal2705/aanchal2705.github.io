About Candies


Candy, also called sweets or lollies, is a confection that features sugar as a principal ingredient. The category, called sugar confectionery, encompasses any sweet confection, including chocolate, chewing gum, and sugar candy. Vegetables, fruit, or nuts which have been glazed and coated with sugar are said to be candied.

TYPES OF CANDIES

  • Caramels

    1. Caramels are made by cooking sugar and water together.
    2. Types of caramel: caramel squares, Coffee Rio, Long Boys, Squirrel nut caramels, Caramel Crunch, Caramel Creams, Jelly Belly Caramel Corn,Vanilla Caramel

  • Hard candies

    1. Hard candies, or boiled sweets, are sugary candies that dissolve slowly in the mouth
    2. Kind of hard candies includes: Candy Buttons, Candy canes, Jolly Rancher, Life Savers, Rock, Sweethearts (candy), PEZ

  • Gummies

    1. Gummies are gelatin based chewy candies that come in a variety of shapes, colors and flavors.
    2. Types of gummies: Gummi bears, Gummi worms, Gummi Cheries, Gummi cola bottles, Gummi fish, Gummi banana strawberry ring, Fraise Tagada
